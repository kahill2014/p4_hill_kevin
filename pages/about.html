About  Brendan  Kaminski

My name is Brendan Kaminski, and I'm an independent warm water fly designer born and raised in Texas. I grew up with an obsession for catching largemouth bass. This obsession started on conventional tackle, throwing soft plastics and jigs. Ten years ago I was introduced to the sport of fly fishing, and a new obsession began!

I didn't buy my first fly tying kit until one fateful day while strolling through a Cabelas, my eyes landed upon a very unique fly in the “Bass fly” bin, the Grim Reaper by Pat Ehlers, a fly based on a conventional bass jig. After seeing this fly, and fishing it thoroughly, I decided to buy my first fly tying kit so that I could tie my own patterns based on proven and effective conventional bass lures.

All of my bass fly designs today take inspiration from people like Pat Ehlers, and the conventional techniques I grew up on. I’m constantly challenging myself to create flies that look as good as they fish, and I can't wait to see what the future holds!
